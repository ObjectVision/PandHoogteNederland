AHN DOWNLOAD

1. Download the downloader at: https://ugetdm.com/downloads/windows/
2. Extract
3. Run Uget-path.reg
4. Open bin/uget.exe
5. Get links from: Brondata/AHN/blad/url_dsm & url_dtm
6. Paste in editor
7. Remove quotes
8. Copy the download links to the clipboard to start the download in Uget
